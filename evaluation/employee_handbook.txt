ACME CORP EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
============================

Section 1: Mission & Core Values
---------------------------------
Acme Corp is committed to excellence, innovation, and integrity in everything we do.
Our core values are Integrity, Innovation, and Teamwork.
Every employee is expected to uphold these values in their daily work and interactions.

Section 2: Work Hours & Remote Policy
--------------------------------------
The standard work week is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time.
Employees may work from home up to two days a week with manager approval.
When working remotely, employees must be available on Slack during standard hours.
All hybrid and remote arrangements must be agreed upon in writing with your manager.

Section 3: Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy
--------------------------------------
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of Paid Time Off per year.
PTO accrues at a rate of 1.25 days per month starting from the first month of employment.
Unused PTO up to 5 days can be rolled over to the next calendar year.
PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance via the HR portal.
Part-time employees accrue PTO on a pro-rated basis.

Section 4: Travel & Expense Policy
------------------------------------
All business travel must be pre-approved by your department manager.
Meals during business travel are capped at $50 per day.
Employees must submit receipts through the Concur expense portal by the 5th of the following month.
Economy class is required for all domestic flights under 5 hours.
Hotel accommodations are reimbursed up to $200 per night in most cities.

Section 5: Code of Conduct
----------------------------
All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct.
Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.
Violations of the code of conduct can result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
Employees must report any witnessed violations to HR within 48 hours.
Retaliation against employees who report violations is strictly prohibited.

Section 6: Benefits & Compensation
-------------------------------------
Acme Corp offers a comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and vision insurance.
The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of the employee's annual salary.
Annual performance reviews are conducted in December each year.
Merit increases are effective January 1st following the annual review.
Employees are eligible for a referral bonus of $2,000 for successful hires.
